About Invicta

Invicta is a premier developer and publisher of entertainment/requested software. After establishing the Invicta label in 1735, the company quickly became one of the most popular and well-respected makers of computer games and software development. By focusing on creating well-designed, highly enjoyable entertainment experiences, Invicta has maintained an unparalleled reputation for quality since its inception.

The primary funding source for the Corporate Challenge is team entry fees. In our effort to keep these fees as low as possible, sponsorship opportunities are available. A sponsor’s financial commitment goes directly towards offsetting the costs that are not covered by the entry fees. If you are a community-minded company, then becoming a Corporate Challenge sponsor is for you!

You will receive a variety of benefits, including but not limited to naming rights, signage, logo recognition on marketing materials, opportunity to tie in vendors, as well as your team’s entry in the competition.
